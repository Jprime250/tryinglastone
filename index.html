🌳 Kakum National Park

A rainforest reserve famous for its canopy walkway and diverse wildlife experiences.

🏖️ Labadi Beach

One of Accra’s most popular beaches known for music, food, and lively weekend vibes.

🌊 Wli Waterfalls

The tallest waterfall in West Africa, hidden in a beautiful green mountain valley.

🏛️ Kwame Nkrumah Museum

Honors Ghana’s first president and tells the story of independence in a peaceful garden setting.

🌊 Nzulezu Stilt Village

A unique village built entirely on stilts over water, accessible only by canoe.

🌉 Adomi Bridge

An iconic steel arch bridge over the Volta River with stunning scenic views.

🪨 Umbrella Rock

A natural rock formation shaped like an umbrella, offering panoramic forest views.

🌉 Kakum National Park

The Canopy Walk at Kakum National Park is a suspended bridge above the rainforest that lets you walk high over the treetops.

🪨 Larabanga Mystic Stone

A stone that refuses to be moved — legend says it returns no matter what. Located near the ancient Larabanga Mosque, this mysterious landmark blends myth, spirituality, and northern Ghana’s rich cultural roots.

🌿 Bobiri Forest & Butterfly Sanctuary

Hidden in the Ashanti Region, Bobiri is a living canvas of color and calm. Walk through shaded trails surrounded by rare butterflies and birds — a soft, magical side of nature many travelers miss.

🕌 Larabanga Mosquef)

The Larabanga Mosque is one of the oldest and most important Islamic landmarks in Ghana, located in the village of Larabanga near Mole National Park in the Savannah Region.
